Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a parking space?

‚ñº
To book a parking space:
1. Select your desired location from the map or list
2. Choose your date, time, and duration
3. Enter your vehicle details
4. Review your booking and proceed to payment
5. You'll receive a confirmation code via email and SMS

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

‚ñº
Yes! You can modify or cancel your booking up to 2 hours before your scheduled arrival time. Go to "My Bookings" and select the booking you want to change. Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% cancellation fee.

What if I arrive late to my booking?

‚ñº
Your parking spot is reserved for 30 minutes after your scheduled time. If you arrive after this grace period, your spot may be reassigned, but we'll do our best to find you an alternative space. We recommend contacting support if you know you'll be late.

Is my payment information secure?

‚ñº
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption (SSL) to protect all payment information. We never store your full card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways.

How do refunds work?

‚ñº
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. For mobile money refunds, the amount is credited back to your account within 24 hours. You'll receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed.

How do I create an account?

‚ñº
Click on "Sign Up" at the top of the page and choose to register as a user or parking space owner. Fill in your details, verify your email address, and you're ready to start booking or listing parking spaces!

How do I reset my password?

‚ñº
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Can I add multiple vehicles to my account?

‚ñº
Yes! You can add unlimited vehicles to your profile. Go to Profile > My Vehicles and click "Add New Vehicle". This makes booking faster as you can select from your saved vehicles.

How do I find my reserved parking spot?

‚ñº
Your booking confirmation includes detailed directions and a spot number. Most locations also have digital displays showing your spot number. Look for signs with your booking code, or ask the attendant who can help you locate your reserved space.

What if my parking spot is occupied?

‚ñº
Contact the on-site attendant or call our support line immediately. We'll either clear the spot within 10 minutes or provide you with an alternative spot at no extra charge. Your satisfaction is guaranteed!

Can I extend my parking duration?

‚ñº
Yes! You can extend your parking time through the app or by contacting support. Additional charges will be calculated based on the hourly rate. Extensions are subject to availability.
